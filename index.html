Attention is fleeting and life is fragile. When I was 18 years old, my world was flipped upside down due to an extreme medical emergency, and I forced to sign consent to operate with a 2% survival rate. To be clear, this 2% meant being a life-long paraplegic. Don’t fret, the outcome was on the contrary, and I’m happy to report that I am 13 years without any limitations. But this experience made me appreciate every aspect of life; and In retrospect, it’s oddly clear as to why I pursued a career in UX. To create incredible experiences that uplift the human condition through digital products.
